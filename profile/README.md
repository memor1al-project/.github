## Our Mission

The Memorial Project is a nonprofit at the intersection of **AI and Holocaust remembrance**. We partner with institutions worldwide — archives, museums, education centers — to transform fragile testimonies into permanent, searchable, educational resources.

As the generation of survivors passes, the urgency of our work compounds. Every transcript, photograph, and recorded voice is a thread connecting the present to one of history's darkest chapters. We are here to hold those threads.

<div align="center">

**Archive & Digitize** | **Animate & Humanize** | **Educate & Distribute**

## Why This Matters

</div>

We are living through a turning point. The last generation of Holocaust survivors and World War II veterans is passing. Within a decade, there will be no living witnesses left to share their stories firsthand. At the same time, misinformation and historical denial are on the rise.

**Technology gives us the means to fight back.**

- **Reconstruct fragmented family histories** from scattered records across dozens of countries
- **Create interactive, immersive experiences** that make history personal and tangible
- **Provide educators with modern tools** to teach the next generation effectively
- **Build permanent digital archives** that will outlast physical documents

## Technology & Infrastructure

</div>

Our technical foundation combines large language models, multimodal AI, graph databases, and cloud archival infrastructure to ensure these records remain accessible — and meaningful — indefinitely.
